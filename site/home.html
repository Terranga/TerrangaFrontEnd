WE ARE TERRANGA

We know that as travelers it’s tough to get off the beaten path; you desire to see more than the typical tour, you want to feel the uniqueness of the city, and meet people that inspire you. As locals, you have so much to offer. You want to share your passions, stories, and love for your city with travelers. And maybe practice a new language!

Terranga helps travelers and locals safely find each other. But we’re not about hiring a local guide or taking tours with locals. It’s about connecting through mutual passions and experiencing what others love to do in their city. With Terranga, there is no need to book. Find out what people love to do in their cities, establish a rapport through messaging and meet to share the experiences with them.

Terranga is unique because we give locals the opportunity to express a dream and travelers the opportunity to say thank you by contributing to their dreams.
