WE ARE TERRANGA

The basic idea is simple (connect two people from different countries). But our goals are huge.

Terranga helps create a shared experience between two or more people who want to discover something new, open their eyes wider, and fill their hearts with gratitude.

We have a name for that: Terranga. The word comes from Senegal, where it is commonly said by strangers passing on the street. It is best translated as camaraderie, acceptance, and greater understanding.

Have you ever met someone whose hospitality overwhelmed you (in a good way)? Or who showed you a perspective you could have never seen on your own? And then you didn't know how you could possibly thank them.

Terranga creates a much-needed way to say THANK YOU. After your adventure, you'll be given the option to contribute to your local partner's dream - which is sometimes an opportunity to travel for the very first time, finish her education, or plant seeds at a community garden.
